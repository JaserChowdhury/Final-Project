How it Started

Origami Yoda. A fantastic tale of a literal paper Yoda was captivating enough on its own, but the origami instructions in the back provided another feature to enjoy. It wasn't long after I put Yoda on my finger that I grew infatuated with origami. Folding paper is such a trivial task that I took great pleasure in the calmness of it. I could spend hour after hour meticulously folding without ever getting tired of it. Everyone needs a stress reliever and origami became mine. Even now, several years later, I continue my work. While I've lost most of my earlier origami, I haven't lost the memories. Nowadays, I've picked up more complex builds, and I'm still learning to this day. Origami is a limitless art, and one that brings true peace of mind at that.
